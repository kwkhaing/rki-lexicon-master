# Audio Recording Guidelines for Rakhine Lexicon

## Recording Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: WAV (uncompressed) preferred, MP3 acceptable
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Bit Depth**: 16-bit or 24-bit
- **Channels**: Mono (preferred) or Stereo
- **Bitrate**: 192 kbps minimum for MP3

### Recording Environment
- Quiet room with minimal echo
- Background noise < -60 dB
- Consistent microphone distance (15-30 cm)
- No plosive pops (use pop filter)

### Speaker Requirements
- Native Rakhine speaker
- Clear articulation
- Natural speaking pace
- Multiple speakers per dialect recommended
